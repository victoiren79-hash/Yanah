Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How do I book a chef?

Click "Hire" on any chef profile. Fill in your event details in the popup form, and the chef will contact you via WhatsApp within 24 hours.

What is the pricing?

Pricing varies by chef and menu. Expect $30-80 per person for ingredients + chef service fee. Payment via Mobile Money after agreement.

Do chefs provide everything?

Yes! Chefs bring fresh ingredients, equipment, and serving platters. They only need access to your kitchen and prep area.
